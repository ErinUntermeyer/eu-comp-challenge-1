date night

movie night

set the scene with extra blankets and pillows. don't forget the popcorn! get extra snacks that wouldn't normally be in the pantry but you'd buy at the theater. pick a movie that means something to your relationship. try to make this night unlike any other normal night watching TV.

at-home campout

adults can make forts too! pull out your camping gear and pitch a tent in your living room. even better, build your own with blankets and chairs! bonus points if you roast some s'mores and tell each other scary stories.

game night

put away the electronics and have some good, quality one-on-one time. board games are fun with a little flirting and light-hearted sh*t talking. bust out the jenga or battleship boards and make some bets... winner gets a massage!

craft party

need some more coffee cups? get some white coffee mugs and paint them together. maybe some new art? experiment with throwing paint on a blank canvas, and each other! creating something together to keep in the house will always remind you of the night you made it.

dance lessons

if you're feeling adventurous, or maybe a little tipsy ;), throw on some You-Tube dance tutorials and learn some new moves. this is a great way to get your heart beating and have some silly fun with your partner.

wine tasting

add to your go-to wine list by having a wine tasting together. get wild and try something new! ballin' on a budget? go to your local liquor store and pick out bottles under $15 of brands you've never had before. oh, and don't forget the cheese!

dessert bake-off

who doesn't love a good cheat-meal? skip dinner and have a dessert bake-off instead! pick a dessert your partner loves and try out a new recipe for it. now you get to enjoy trying each others creations!

do a puzzle

clear the living room and grab some pillows, it's puzzle night! pick something with medium-difficulty so you can have some fun and focus on each other too. when you're done, you can even save the memory by turning it into art!
